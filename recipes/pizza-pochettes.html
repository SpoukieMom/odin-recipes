Pizza Pochettes


Description

Pizza Pochettes are a delicious snack or meal option, perfect for any occasion. They are filled with cheese and pepperoni, all wrapped in a crispy crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pizza pochettes
  • 1 essuie tout
  • Micro-onde

Steps

  1. Take the pizza pochettes out of the box.
  2. Place them on a plate lined with essuie tout.
  3. Put them in the micro-onde.
  4. Heat them for 2-3 minutes or until hot.
  5. Let them cool for a minute before serving.
